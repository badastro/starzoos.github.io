    True or False: Summer holidays edition
    7 Questions
    A lot went on in Harry’s summer holiday, but what do you recall about those sunny days? Can you separate the truth from the lies?
    QUESTION 1/7
    The first Hogwarts letter posted to Privet Drive arrived in June.
    QUESTION 2/7
    Mr and Mrs Mason came for dinner at the Dursleys when it was also Harry’s birthday.
    QUESTION 3/7
    Harry, Hermione, the Weasleys and the Diggorys used an old kettle as a Portkey to travel to the Quidditch World Cup.
    QUESTION 4/7
    Bulgaria scored 160 points at the Quidditch World Cup.
    QUESTION 5/7
    In the Order of the Phoenix book, Harry and Dudley were attacked by two Dementors when walking home on a summer evening.
    QUESTION 6/7
    On Harry’s first evening at Grimmauld Place, Molly made a roast chicken dinner for everyone.

    When playing two-a-side Quidditch at The Burrow during the summer before sixth-year, Hermione was Harry’s teammate.
