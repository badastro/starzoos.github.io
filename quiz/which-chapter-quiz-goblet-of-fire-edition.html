    Which chapter quiz: Goblet of Fire edition
    10 Questions
    Harry Potter certainly packed a lot into his fourth year at Hogwarts. So how much Triwizardry can you recall from Goblet of Fire, the book that introduced us to Mad-Eye Moody, Rita Skeeter, Viktor Krum, Fleur Delacour and (sob) Cedric Diggory?
    QUESTION 1/10
    When did Harry send off his letter to Sirius, to tell him about his scar hurting?
    QUESTION 2/10
    What was the name of the chapter which featured Rita Skeeter’s report into activities at the Quidditch World Cup?
