    Hiko Waldew

    Born into royalty on the aquatic planet Humedon, Hiko’s life is upended by a war on water, forcing him and his mother, Auda, to flee to the remote moon Jogi. In a series of accidents after turning twelve, he would eventually leave his world, discover his spiritual connection to Kaleidons, and embark upon many cosmic adventures with his shape-shifting kotteroo, Roodew, robot Jamini, and twin best friends, Belka and Strelka Muttnik.
