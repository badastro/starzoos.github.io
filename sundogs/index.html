    THE FOUNDER
    Mona Zemut
    The movement’s origins trace back to the end of the Thousand Years’ War. Shokusans and inventors Mona Zemut and Paca Relot created ZooStars and ZooKeys to harness Kaleidon power, helping Stars United win. After the war, the duo founded Sun-Moon StarZoo, a galaxy-wide network intended to protect Kaleidons and educate the public. She defected after a personal tragedy and founded Sundogs.
