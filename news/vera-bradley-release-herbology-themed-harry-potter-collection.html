    Dig into Vera Bradley’s new Herbology-inspired accessory range

    Published on Apr 20th 2021
    USUS and Canada-based shoppers are invited to embrace the greenery of the wizarding world with this new Harry Potter x Vera Bradley collection - out now.

    From floral robes to cute tote bags, channel your inner Professor Sprout with this beautiful new collection from American brand, Vera Bradley.

    The Hogwarts lesson Herbology is very much the theme for this group of bags, charms and blankets, with magical plants such as Mandrakes and Devil’s Snare featuring in the designs, immersed in illustrated floral patterns.

    So, if you’re the green-fingered type and based in the US or Canada, have a browse of the collection today.

    Vera Bradley has numerous other Harry Potter-themed products to peruse, such as Hogwarts house themed bags and cute plush blankets. As the spring and summer months kick in, this could be the perfect seasonal look for Harry Potter fans to treat themselves to.

    Visit the official Vera Bradley website for more details.
