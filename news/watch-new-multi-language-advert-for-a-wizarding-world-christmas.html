    New multi-language advert celebrates a Wizarding World Christmas

    Published on Dec 13th 2022
    ThisThis new advert features families from all around the world getting settled in for a #WizardingWorldChristmas! Here’s our handy guide to all the products you see.

    From Harry Potter decorations on Christmas trees to Wizarding World stockings, everything in this video features Harry Potter products! Take a look at this cool new take on ‘Twas the Night Before Christmas, performed in different languages, to really show the worldwide love of Harry Potter. And you might get a few late Christmas shopping tips too!

    So, what products did you spot that you had your eye on? We have collected the entire list for you to peruse - although if anything is missing, they may have all been snapped up already!

    Primark - Harry Potter Hogwarts Sweatshirt

    Get cosy with this lovely green number from Primark - you'll be the bell of the Christmas dinner in this.

    Modern Gourmet/Thoughtfully - Harry Potter 12 Days of Hot Chocolate Bombs

    This must-have Christmas hot chocolate gift for Harry Potter fans, includes 12 cocoa bombs in Harry Potter packaging and one wand stirrer. The flavors in each hot chocolate advent calendar include three Caramel Hot Cocoa Bombs, three Strawberry Hot Cocoa Bombs, three S’mores Hot Cocoa Bombs, and three Original Milk Chocolate Cocoa Bombs.

    Target AUS - Harry Potter Hogwarts Lip Gloss Set

    This 4-pack lip gloss set in shades of pink features each Hogwarts house emblem.

    Target AUS - Harry Potter Hogwarts Shimmer Bath Bomb Set

    These Harry Potter Bath Bombs come in a set of 4. Drop one into a bath of warm water and let the fragrance invigorate your senses.

    Target AUS - Harry Potter Hogwarts 4Pk Lip Balm Set

    This 4-pack lip balm features each Hogwarts house and their emblem.

    Target AUS - Harry Potter Bath Tin Bauble

    A Platform 9 3/4 bauble containing two mini bath bombs inside.

    Lionel - Lionel Harry Potter Hogwarts Express Mini Model Train Set

    Battery powered Hogwarts Express mini model train set with authentic train sounds and announcements.

    Homesick - Harry Potter Gryffindor™ Candle

    This scarlet and gold candle comes with notes of smoked cedar and cinnamon.

    Homesick - Harry Potter Hufflepuff™ Candle

    This candle holds the comforting aromas of chamomile and burnt sugar.

    Homesick - Harry Potter Ravenclaw™ Candle

    Homesick - Harry Potter Slytherin™ Candle

    LEGO - Harry Potter Advent Calendar

    Delight Harry Potter™ fans with daily surprises, including 7 minifigures and iconic items from the Harry Potter movies.

    Lovepop - Harry Potter Happy Christmas Wreath

    This 3D paper wreath has an array of laser-cut leaves in various shapes and shades of green, making it full and lush. Lit candles appear to flicker around the wreath, almost as if floating in the Great Hall at Hogwarts™. Harry and Ron's iconic Christmas sweaters sit on the wreath, one blue with an 'H' and one red with an 'R.' Baubles hang among the other decorations, featuring the crests of each Hogwarts™ house. Snowflakes, stars, and holly berries pepper the wreath adding even more holiday spirit.

    Popsockets - PopWallet+ Gold Foil Hermione’s Notebook

    This sleek, detailed PopWallet carries your cards on your phone, with a Wizarding World flourish.

    Popsockets - Hermione's Notebook

    A slim protective design that's certified to protect your phone for drops up to ten feet. Well, seeing as we can't use the Levitating Charm, this will work nicely!

    Popsockets - Enamel Time Turner

    Complete with adorable Time-Turner design so you can live your best Hermione-life.

    MinaLima - The Magic of MinaLima

    Celebrate Mira and Eduardo's twenty-year journey with this magical compendium chronicling their extraordinary partnership designing for the Wizarding World. Our immersive book is bursting with images, graphics and unseen sketches plus original commentary and interactive elements.

    Pottery Barn Teen - Harry Potter Antique Ornaments (set of 3)

    Add magical style to your tree with our enchanting Harry Potter holiday ornaments. The keepsakes are hand painted in antiqued gold and finished with a luxe, velvet hanging ribbon.

    Pottery Barn Kids - Harry Potter™ Stocking Collection

    Add a touch of magic to your mantel with these Harry Potter Christmas stockings. These exclusive designs from the beloved series are a luxe addition to your decor that lets you show off favorite characters, icons and House pride.

    Pottery Barn Teen - Harry Potter™ Time-Turner™ Clock

    Time flies, especially when you’re time traveling! Get to wherever you need to go on time with help from our Time-Turner Clock complete with gyroscopic and hourglass detailing. Featuring a gilded and spellbinding design, this Harry Potter clock is a magical addition to your room's decor.

    Le Creuset - Hogwarts™ Houses Dessert Plate Set

    Curated for the Gryffindor, Slytherin, Hufflepuff or Ravenclaw in all of us, the Hogwarts ™ Houses Dessert Plate Set captures the anticipation of feasts in the Great Hall and friendly competitions between loyal, intelligent, cunning and brave students!

    Le Creuset - Hogwarts™ Houses Potholder

    Like the colourful uniforms at Hogwarts, this house-themed potholder combines a quad of colors with a gold-threaded, embroidered crest. The essence of Hogwarts is stitched into every golden “H” and designed from durable, printed cotton canvas and blue terry cloth.

    Funko - Harry Potter 2022 Advent Calendar

    Open the tiny doors to reveal 24 unique Funko Pocket Pops! featuring Harry and his friends. Place them in front of the packaging’s artwork to create an enchanting scene in Diagon Alley.

    Marks & Spencer - Harry Potter™ Pyjamas (6-16 Yrs)

    Treat Harry Potter™ fans to these magical pure cotton pyjamas. This comfy regular fit pair includes a long sleeve top with a crest on the chest and a Hogwarts slogan down the arm. The checked bottoms have an elasticated waist and cuffs for a snug feel.

    Zavvi T- Harry Potter Hogwarts Crest Men's T-Shirt – Navy

    This creative Harry Potter Hogwarts Crest Men's T-Shirt is a one-of-a-kind product, exclusive to Zavvi.

    Wow! Stuff - Harry Potter Invisibility Cloak

    Vanish from view with this amazing Harry Potter toy and collector item. The perfect Harry Potter dress up or cosplay for kids, adults and Harry Potter fans of all ages.

    HGTV - 13" Black Tie Arrangement

    HGTV - 60" Black Tie Mantel Swag
