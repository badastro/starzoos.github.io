    An exclusive look inside the MinaLima edition of Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

    Published on Feb 9th 2021
    GraphicGraphic design studio MinaLima created the vision for the Harry Potter and Fantastic Beasts films. Now, they’re back again with their latest take on the Wizarding World. Coming this October, take a look at their new interpretation of Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets, packed with beautiful new illustrations!

    A stunning new edition of Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets will be hitting bookshelves on 26th October this year – designed by MinaLima. Here is your first look at the cover.

    This new visual interpretation of the second Harry Potter adventure, which follows Harry as he tries to decipher the mysteries of the Chamber of Secrets at Hogwarts, will be filled with colour illustrations and interactive paper craft pages.

    Fans can expect to see new illustrations of the Weasley home, The Burrow, and everyone’s favourite bad-tempered tree, The Whomping Willow. Fans will even be able to interact with elements of the book, such as pulling some crying Mandrakes out of their pots!

    Here’s a look at some of the beautiful illustrations you can expect to see.

    This new illustrated edition follows MinaLima’s work on the first book, Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone/Philosopher’s Stone, which you can take a look at right here.

    The MinaLima Edition of Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets is coming out on hardback on 26th October and will be released by Scholastic in the US and Bloomsbury in the UK.
