    Every single voice you hear in Harry Potter: The Full-Cast Audio Editions

    Published on Oct 30th 2025
    WhenWhen we say the new Harry Potter audiobook series has a full cast we’re not joking! From Albus Dumbledore to Petunia Dursley, discover the voice behind every single character in this star-studded production.

    With five audiobooks from this spellbinding series by Pottermore and Audible now available, we thought it was the perfect time to get to know the talented cast. We’ll let a few introduce themselves…

    The sheer talent in that video alone was jaw dropping and that’s just the tip of the iceberg! There are so many brilliant actors whose voices have helped to shape the series – hundreds in fact!

    We wanted to make sure every single person was recognised so we’ve pulled together a list of all the voices you hear in the audiobooks. Having started with the cast of Philosopher’s Stone, with each release, we will be updating this article to include all the curious characters we meet along the way.

    So, please welcome the wonderful voices joining the cast in Order of the Phoenix.
