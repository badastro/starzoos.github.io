    Build-A-Bear workshop launches Wizarding World-inspired range

    Published on Aug 31st 2020
    TheThe Build-A-Bear company, in partnership with Warner Bros. Consumer Products, have a very adorable offering coming your way on 2nd September: a new Wizarding World collection.

    We love flaunting our Hogwarts house in all sorts of ways – and now – you can dress up your very own Wizarding World themed Build-A-Bear to join you for the first time ever.

    The Build-A-Bear workshop, which specialises in customisable teddies, has joined the Wizarding World for a special collaboration, giving you the opportunity to create your grizzly wizard friend. You’ll be able to accessorise your bear with their own Hogwarts robe, a woven sweater, (Hogwarts can get cold, after all!) a Hogwarts house-themed tie, a scarf, and, of course, a wand. Bear-sized wands, naturally. Maybe they can cast Expecto Paw-tronum with them.

    The bears are available in black or brown colours with golden paws, and even come with a special wand wrist accessory.

    There will also be a special one-of-a-kind Harry Potter gift box that you can purchase, to give a fellow Wizarding World fan a very cute surprise.

    For more information about Build-A-Bear, visit their site, and follow the brand on Facebook, Twitter, YouTube and Instagram. The new Wizarding World range will be on sale from 2nd September, starting exclusively online.
