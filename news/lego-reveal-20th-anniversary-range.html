    LEGO reveal 20th anniversary product range

    Published on Apr 15th 2021
    CelebrateCelebrate 20 years of LEGO Harry Potter with their anniversary collection of products which are set to be in store from June 1st.

    LEGO have announced that they are releasing a number of new sets celebrating 20 years of LEGO Harry Potter.

    From the 16th April you will be able to pre-order these charming new sets which feature everything from Harry’s first flying lesson in Philosopher’s Stone to brewing Polyjuice Potion in Chamber of Secrets. There are even large-scale versions of beloved characters such as Hermione Granger and Harry Potter which stand at 10 inches tall!

    We particularly love the exclusive golden minifigures that come with some of the LEGO Harry Potter sets and believe that this captivating collection has something for fans of all ages.

    Why not let your imagination run wild and try to get past the obstacles that guard the Philosopher’s Stone? Can your minifigures sneak past Fluffy and get to the trapdoor? Do they have what it takes to win at a game of wizard chess?

    Maybe you would like to explore the Chamber of Secrets from Harry’s second year. Can your minifigures get past Tom Riddle and the Basilisk? Or spend some time building Dumbledore’s majestic phoenix Fawkes. This 597-piece phoenix model even moves! Turn the handle at the back and watch his wings go up and down.

    If you fancy an adventure outside of Hogwarts, you can take a trip to snowy Hogsmeade. Here you could have a look round Honeydukes or sip a Butterbeer at the Three Broomsticks.

    Some of these wonderful LEGO Harry Potter sets can be pre-ordered from the 16th April at LEGO.com and are available to buy from the 1st June online and in selected stores.
