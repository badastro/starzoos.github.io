    Learn all about the Harry Potter publishing program for Spring 2021

    Published on Mar 24th 2021
    WhetherWhether it’s an exploration of Diagon Alley or new deep-dives into your Hogwarts house, this new selection of books find innovative ways to celebrate the Wizarding World. Take a look.

    New collectible titles are coming soon, all celebrating the stories of Harry Potter in their own unique ways. Take a look at what’s in store this Spring.

    Harry Potter: Gryffindor Magic: Artifacts from the Wizarding World & Harry Potter: Slytherin Magic: Artifacts from the Wizarding World – Insight Editions

    For the Gryffindors and Slytherins among you, Insight Editions is publishing a new series of books all themed around your Hogwarts houses. Expect movie trivia, never-before-seen art, behind-the-scenes film facts and interesting titbits about your house that you might not have known.

    These books also come with deluxe cases full of extra surprises. Unfold the case to reveal a house pennant, button, patches and pencils – so you can step up your stationary game while you study up on your house!

    Hufflepuff and Ravenclaw editions will be available June 29th.

    You can learn more about the Gryffindor book here and the Slytherin book here.

    Harry Potter Spiroglyphics

    Now a treat for all you budding artists out there. Harry Potter Spiroglyphics, created by best-selling author Thomas Pavitte, invites you to discover iconic wizarding world characters inside these clever graphic puzzles.

    Simply look deep into the spiral creations, start to colour between the lines, and your favourite Harry Potter characters will gradually appear from your felt-tip pen. So not just an artistic exercise, but also a very therapeutic one.

    Learn more about the book here.

    Harry Potter: Exploring Diagon Alley – Insight Editions

    We’ve always longed to roam the cobbled streets of Diagon Alley and do some magical window shopping. This new book, Exploring Diagon Alley, invites you to get close to that experience – analysing every facet of the wizarding shopping district from the films.

    Examine immersive drawings of the street, look out for iconic movie scenes, and delve deep into every wizarding shop and establishment Diagon Alley has to offer, from Ollivander’s wand shop to Weasleys’ Wizard Wheezes.

    Learn more about the book here.

    Harry Potter: Crafting Wizardry – Insight Editions

    If you consider yourself a crafty type – you might learn some new tips thanks to this new book, Crafting Wizardry. This family-friendly guide invites you to craft your own wand, make your own Harry Potter-themed decorations for the home and divulges handy advice on how to creatively showcase your Hogwarts house pride in fresh ways.

    You can also get inspired with concept art from the films and behind-the-scenes insights into the movie making wizardry of Harry Potter. Maybe in your future, you could be a set designer or a prop maker and create your own magic!

    Learn more about the book here.
