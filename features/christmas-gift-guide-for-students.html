    Harry Potter Christmas gift guide for students

    Published on Oct 22nd 2024

    Festive Harry Potter sock 3-pack

    You know what Professor Dumbledore says: one can never have enough socks. And these winter warmers from the Harry Potter shop are the perfect easy side-present. Each pair comes in a different, cheery design, adorned with Christmas trees and the beloved platform nine and three-quarters logo for that subtle wizarding world nod.

    Harry Potter: Quidditch Champions

    This high-flying game is the latest release from Portkey Games, inviting fans to take to the skies and perform in your very own Quidditch tournament. Available on PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, Steam and Epic Games Store, this gift will surely have the recipient levitating with glee.

    Take a look.

    Harry Potter travel neck pillow

    Know someone who’s planning for a big trip? Why not give them a smidge of the wizarding world to take along with them? These enchanting neck pillows from the Harry Potter Shop are part of their colourful travel range – from luggage tags to passport covers. The fun scrapbook style design is full of wizarding world references, with winks at Hogsmeade, Hagrid’s motorbike and the Knight Bus.

    UK-based shoppers will love some of Boots’ Harry Potter goodies - and these hanging Hogwarts house themed banners could be perfect for a student wanting to dress up their bedroom like a retro-style school dormitory.

    This sunny apricot-coloured range from the Harry Potter shop is your way of representing your Harry Potter love in a cute, casual way. From hoodies to tees, we’ve picked out the cap – which comes emblazoned with iconic symbols from the Harry Potter stories – from the lightning bolt to the Deathly Hallows emblem.

    Another cute contribution from the UK Boots brand – these lunch bags are perfectly spacious for your break-time, and come in four different colours, per Hogwarts house, naturally. You can also remind yourself of your key Hogwarts house traits, which are featured in the design.

    Harry Potter snack containers

    These sleekly designed snack containers are perfect for the busy student on the go. Complete with Harry Potter iconography and friendly pastel colours, pop your Every Flavour Beans in here and you’ll be on your way.

    LEGO® Harry Potter™ Hogwarts™ Icons - Collectors' Edition

    This special piece from the LEGO® would truly give someone the most magical Christmas ever. Celebrating our favourite snowy owl, Hedwig, complete with an all-important Hogwarts letter (which you can personalise, by the way) potions bottles, a Golden Snitch and even Harry’s glasses, this epic model comes with over 3000 pieces, and has everything a Harry Potter fan could possibly ask for.

    Conquest journals and planners

    At Christmas time, we celebrate the year just passed and look ahead to the future. These beautiful planners from Conquest Journals could help you organise your weeks with a bit of Harry Potter flair. With colourful sticker sheets and whimsical illustrations, here’s to a far-more organised 2025!

    Take a look.

    LAMY x Harry Potter fountain pens

    Now that you have your journals, you’re going to need something to write in! We are particularly keen on this classy LAMY range - a German brand that creates the most sophisticated writing instruments.

    This Harry Potter-themed collection of fountain pens comes in Hogwarts house colours, designed with the school emblem at the cap. Well, if we can’t have quills, this is the next best thing.
