    Watch the new episode from the Discover Harry Potter series and see the mysteries of Hogwarts explained with LEGO® Harry Potter sets

    Published on Sep 1st 2022
    TheThe secrets of Hogwarts castle seem never-ending, but thankfully, our friends at LEGO® have helped us piece together some of those mysteries, brick by brick.

    In our new series, Discover Harry Potter, we’ve been helping wizarding world newcomers learn all about Harry Potter with these fun new explainer videos. (You can look at the episodes so far here) Today, we have an extra special episode, all acted out via LEGO® sets! Take a look.

    This video features some new LEGO® Harry Potter™ Hogwarts sets for you to get excited about, including Dumbledore’s Office and the Magical Trunk. Also check out glimpses of some sets you may recognise, such as the Chamber of Secrets and the Whomping Willow.

    Of course, this video is simply the tip of the iceberg when it comes to your wizarding world education. Be sure to check out the rest of our Discover Harry Potter series on YouTube over the coming weeks and catch up on older episodes today. Do you have someone in your life who doesn’t know their Muggles from their Mugwumps? This series is especially for them, and perfect to start with during our Back to Hogwarts season.

    More Discover Harry Potter videos will be out soon.

    You can visit the LEGO® website to see which Harry Potter characters, sets and locations are available in brick form, including a few new goodies especially for 2022's Back to Hogwarts season.
