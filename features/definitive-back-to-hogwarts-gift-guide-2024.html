    Your definitive Back to Hogwarts gift guide for 2024

    Published on Aug 15th 2024
    RollRoll up, roll up: we have new LEGO! We have a cute kidswear range! We have many, many enchanting gift ideas for Back to Hogwarts season 2024. Take a look.

    Back to Hogwarts season is kind of like our version of Christmastime – we celebrate, we get merry with fans – there’s even a New Years’ Eve-style countdown on 1st September.

    In the spirit of Back to Hogwarts, we heartily encourage you to commemorate it with that special Harry Potter fan in your life; and what better way to do that than with a little present or two. Check out our ultimate gift guide for 2024, where we’ve gathered up all of the brightest and most magical things available.

    Special Amazon offer

    Firstly, a special start-of-term announcement from Amazon! Come experience the magic of Harry Potter - from LEGO sets to an interactive Hedwig - at special prices, starting from today - running 'til 9th of September. See what you can find - available in UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain.

    Playtime magic

    Maybe you’re an avid collector – or maybe you’re looking for a special knick-knack for a younger Harry Potter fan in your life. This selection of adorable Harry Potter-themed toys should help give you some inspiration.

    Harry Potter Compact | Polly Pocket

    It's Hogwarts in miniature! And it’s absolutely charming. Yes, the good folks down at Polly Pocket HQ have cast “Reducio!” on your Harry Potter favourites, creating these very cute wizarding world vignettes, all packed together in this handy compact. 5 micro-dolls and 11 accessories can be found inside, celebrating your favourite locations and characters.

    Take a look at Polly Pocket

    LionChief | Hogwarts Express train set

    This amazing detailed depiction of the famous scarlet train comes with Bluetooth 5.0 voice control, multiple locomotive cars and tracks and a controller to set your train in motion! Train collectors: we may have found your dream Back to Hogwarts gift to choo-choo-choose.

    All aboard!

    Double double, toys and cuddles

    The wizarding world is full of weird and wonderful things: but sometimes we just want something cute and cuddly. This selection of plushies and soft toys are some of our favourites from the bountiful options available.

    Build-a-Bear x Harry Potter

    What’s better than one Hogwarts-house-themed Build-a-Bear?

    A teeny tiny Build-a-Bear that sits on their shoulder, that’s what! Yes, you can pick up the iconic Build-a-Bear teddies in accordance with your Hogwarts house, but you can now also check out these mini-bear gift sets that come with a tiny companion, coming in green, blue, red and yellow, depending on which Hogwarts house you choose. Other bears in the collection come with actual Hogwarts acceptance letters too!

    Plushies | Harry Potter Shop

    Over at the Harry Potter shop, you could pick up a traditional familiar (such as Hermione’s iconic orange cat Crookshanks) or go for something more off-piste, such as a Hungarian Horntail plushie. We’re sure Hagrid thinks they’re adorable – maybe you do too? You could even get a Hagrid plushie to go alongside, if you worry about it being lonely.

    If you got the reference up above – well done. Yes, this is the books section! When it comes to gifting a Harry Potter fan absolutely anything, sometimes it’s best to take inspiration from Hermione. Here’s some of our favourite recent titles and editions that are needing a good home to go to.

    Artifacts from the Wizarding World | Hogwarts house editions

    With each Hogwarts house comes a great story. These new books, written by Jody Revenson for Insight Editions, come in four coloured covers, corresponding to Gryffindor, Ravenclaw, Hufflepuff and Slytherin. Learn trivia you might not have known about YOUR Hogwarts house, discover beautiful concept art and learn behind-the-scenes movie facts. Each book comes with cute pull-out collectibles, such as fabric patches, postcards and bookmarks.
