    Watch episode two of Hogwarts Hangout: What if Neville had been the Chosen One?

    Published on Nov 8th 2024
    It’sIt’s time for a brand-new episode of our web series Hogwarts Hangout! Featuring two of our very own editorial team, it’s time to geek out with you about our favourite Harry Potter topics. This time, we want to discuss what if our favourite underdog, Neville Longbottom, had been the Chosen One.

    Welcome to episode two of Hogwarts Hangout featuring Annie and Reece, two of our editorial team. Today they are talking all things Neville from his clumsy beginnings to his heroic glow-up. Most importantly, they’re discussing what happened to Neville’s pet toad Trevor. Discover the answer to that mystery here…

    What do you think? Do you agree with Annie and Reece? Or do you have a rather different opinion. Whether you think you think Neville never had what it took to defeat Voldemort or always thought he would be a Horcrux-destroying, sword-swinging star, we want to know.

    If you enjoyed this episode, don’t forget to visit our Harry Potter YouTube channel for future episodes or to catch up on our first episode where we dived into the world of the Marauders. If you’re old enough, don’t forget to let us know what you think in the comments.

    You can check out more of our videos at our official Harry Potter YouTube channel too.
