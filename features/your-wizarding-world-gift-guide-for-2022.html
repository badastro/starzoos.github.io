    Your ultimate Wizarding World Christmas gift guide for 2022

    Published on Nov 14th 2022
    TheThe festive season is upon us! And this year, we absolutely promise to do our Christmas shopping early and not leave it ‘til last minute. Definitely. To help you do the same, here’s a handy gift guide for that huge Harry Potter fan in your life.

    Oh, what to get… the possibilities are endless. And although we don’t have Hermione’s enchanted beaded bag to fit in absolutely everything, here’s a few ideas, from festive knits to pretty things for the home.

    A bit of light reading

    Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix: Illustrated Edition

    This year, the fifth instalment of Bloomsbury’s Illustrated Edition series was released, celebrating the visual stylings of Jim Kay and Neil Packer. This time around, you can enjoy new artistic interpretations of the characters introduced to us in Order of the Phoenix, from the dastardly Dolores Umbridge to the delightful Luna Lovegood, and immerse yourselves in some of the book’s epic moments, such as the assembling of Dumbledore’s Army or the showdown in the Department of Mysteries

    Pick up the book here from Bloomsbury's website.

    The Magic of MinaLima: Celebrating the Graphic Design Studio Behind the Harry Potter & Fantastic Beasts Films

    The graphic design team behind the Harry Potter and Fantastic Beasts films are looking back at two decades worth of hard work, bringing the Wizarding World to life in all sorts of ways. This new compendium gives you a behind-the-scenes looks at the most memorable Wizarding World props and pieces, such as the Daily Prophet, Marauder’s Map and more.

    Visit MinaLima's official website to check out the book where you could pick up a hand-signed copy.

    Something pretty

    Nordic Christmas Throw

    This year, the Harry Potter shop has a delightful Nordic Christmas collection to browse – absolutely perfect for those who like a minimalistic style and ideal for these winter months. This heavy weight, cosy green fleece would be just splendid for a snuggle on the sofa. More like Hygge-warts rather than Hogwarts, you might say!

    Take a look at it here.

    Something epic

    Nordic Christmas Trunk / Joyeux Noel Christmas Trunk

    If you’re looking for something extra-special this year, try out these Nordic Christmas trunks, which will sate the most dedicated of Harry Potter fans, complete with a unique style. Very capacious extremis!

    Take a look!

    Harry Potter: Magic Caster Wand

    This upcoming product boasts the “ultimate wand experience” – and will allow you to create real magic in your home with a variety of wizarding world spells to learn through a companion app. Use the wand and app to interact with smart devices to create an immersive, multi-sensory experience!

    Available in different designs, your Magic Caster Wand will help you become the skilled witch or wizard you’ve always wanted to be. Swish and flick your way to greatness!

    You can pre-order right here.

    Festive fashion

    Deck the Great Hall / Christmas Spirit Jersey

    This friendly and sparkly sweatshirt boasts all four Hogwarts houses, complete with a brilliant Great Hall Christmas tree. Cosy and colourful, you’ll be the life of the Harry Potter party in this little number.

    Take a look.

    Nordic Adult Christmas pyjamas

    Is it really Christmas without a new set of pyjamas? Surely not. This set, boasting the new festive Nordic design in a lovely shade of pine-green, are quite simply adorable. You get extra cosy bonus points if you grab these.

    Snap them up here.

    Joyeux Noel Knitted Christmas

    Come on, admit it, we all love a cheesy, retro Christmas jumper – and this one is particularly merry. Complete with glasses and lightning bolt motifs alongside the traditional snowflakes, this vintage style knit is extremely charming. Would Mrs Weasley be jealous?

    Experiences

    Warner Bros Studio Tour London Gift Tickets

    Perhaps you are a terrible gift-wrapper and simply want to get someone a nice, easy voucher. A highly relatable feeling. Sometimes an experience might be more preferable than an object, and this one from Warner Bros. Studio Tour London might be just the ticket.

    A trip to the Studio Tour is a guaranteed magical day out – with the space now boasting a more bountiful selection of displays than ever before, from a spooky Forbidden Forest, to a resplendent Gringotts bank.

    Have a look at the website's latest offerings here.

    Harry Potter: A Yule Ball Celebration (Milan, Montreal, Houston, Mexico City)

    New for this year is an exciting pop-up Yule Ball experience, which promises a day of dancing, feasting and fancy festivities. If you know someone who lives in the four territories of the Yule Ball experience, Milan, Montreal, Houston or Mexico City, you could gift them their own personal Triwizard Tournament fantasy.

    Learn more about it at the official website.

    A trip to see Harry Potter and the Cursed Child

    The magic of the eighth Harry Potter story has traversed all over the world, with productions of the hit play currently open in London, New York, Melbourne, Hamburg, Toronto and Tokyo. Visit the global website to look at ticket info and bring a loved one for an unforgettable day out at the theatre.

    Be sure to check our Discover Experiences section of the website, if you fancy having a browse of other real-life Harry Potter adventures you could partake in!

    Stocking fillers

    Joeux Noel Cookie Jar

    If you have a little cookie monster in your life, this cookie jar with Quidditch stylings could be a great little gift for them. It has a Snitch on the lid!! Need we say more?

    Take a look.

    Nordic Tea Light Dome

    This beautiful snowy white tea-light is Hedwig-themed, and would surely be the pride of your mantelpiece. Ornate, clean and classic, this would be the perfect little stocking filler for those house-proud Harry Potter fans.

    Available right this way.

    Joeux Noel Jumper Mug Set

    Joeux Noel Jumper Mug Set

    Is your day punctuated by multiple cups of tea? We completely understand. And you know what your cup of tea needs in these cold, winter months? A cute little Mrs Weasley-style jumper! We are, quite frankly, obsessed. This set provides gorgeous embellished cups, and then a little Harry or Ron cosy to go over the top.

    Here's where to get your own.

    For the kids

    LEGO® Harry Potter™: Holidays at Hogwarts™

    LEGO® + Christmas. They just go together like cheese and crackers. New for this year, this lovely illustrated book (for ages 6 and up) might be a lovely gift for a young one. The book contains many festive facts about the wizarding world, with full-colour spreads of your favourite characters and locations, all immortalized in LEGO®, of course. This gift also comes with a little Harry Potter minifigure, dressed for Christmas in his dapper Yule Ball robes.

    Kyte Baby x Harry Potter collection

    Texas-based baby and toddler clothing company Kyte have launched a new Harry Potter collaboration, boasting a number of Wizarding World-themed pieces in soft bamboo, perfect for sensitive baby’s skin. Three prints are used across the products, including Flight, which focuses on flying creatures, Herbology, which uses magical plant motifs and Icons, which looks at the most memorable Harry Potter iconography, such as the Golden Snitch, Deathly Hallows symbol and lightning bolt scar. Extremely cute, soft and snuggly.

    Take a gander at the website.

    Wizarding World Harry Potter, Magical Minis Diagon Alley 3-in-1 Playset

    This Wizarding World playset is the perfect setting for your adventures, with three Diagon Alley shops included: Weasleys’ Wizard Wheezes, Eeylops Owl Emporium and Florian Fortescue’s Ice Cream Parlour. Featuring two exclusive 3- inch Hermione Granger and Fred Weasley dolls, 21 accessories, and lights and sounds.

    Here's a closer look.
