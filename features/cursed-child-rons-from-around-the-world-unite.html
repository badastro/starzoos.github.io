    Watch the worldwide cast of Harry Potter and the Cursed Child celebrate their favourite Ron moments

    Published on Feb 28th 2021
    TodayToday marks Ron Weasley's birthday, and to celebrate, the various Rons from Cursed Child productions across the globe joined together (virtually) to celebrate their character's special day. Take a look.

    Thomas Aldridge, Paul Thornley, David Abeles, Michael Whalley and Sebastian Witt are just a few of the talented actors from all around the world who have donned the famous ginger hair to portray Ron Weasley in Harry Potter and the Cursed Child.

    In the video below, the actors who portray Ron in those various worldwide productions (you'll see London, Hamburg, Melbourne, New York and San Francisco all represented) have virtually united to celebrate their character's birthday. A Ron-dezvous, if you will.

    Enjoy, as the cast regale us with some of their favourite Ron moments from the Harry Potter books.
