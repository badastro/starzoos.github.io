    Which Weasley jumper would Molly knit you for Christmas?

    Published on Dec 10th 2025
    FromFrom knitted dragons to maroon masterpieces, it wouldn’t be Christmas without a Weasley jumper! Lovingly handmade by Molly, which of these one-of-a-kind knits would suit your personality? Let’s find out…

    As someone who believes family is everything… you’d have Harry’s first Weasley jumper

    Having felt like an outsider for most of his life, it was an incredibly moving moment when Harry got his first Weasley jumper. That bundle of wool meant he was now a part of the Weasley family. He had found his people.

    Being part of a family is something that is important to you. While that can mean your closest relations, it also applies to those you choose to be your nearest and dearest too. They are the people you turn to for support, your biggest cheerleaders and you never take them for granted. In fact, you would do anything for them, which is why this jumper is perfect for you.

    Sometimes struggle to have your voice heard? You’d get Ron’s maroon knit

    Every single Christmas, Ron had the same complaint… his Weasley jumper was maroon. Even though he believed his dislike of the colour was obvious to all around him, his mum never seemed to get the memo. This is something you relate to.

    While it might not be a jumper, every year you can rely on that one relative to get you a present which is just not your vibe. And every year, you fake a smile and say thank you. You keep the peace and avoid drama at all costs… even if you wish things were different.

    However, unless they’re a gifted Seer, people aren’t mind readers. If you don’t tell them how you feel, how will they know? Did Ron ever directly tell his mother about his maroon aversion? We don’t think so. If he had, perhaps she would have made him the Weasley jumper of his dreams. If you make this the year where you find that inner-courage and speak out... perhaps you could end up with the Christmas present of your dreams too?

    Is living a quiet life your worst nightmare? Harry’s Hungarian Horntail jumper would suit you

    Nothing makes you happier than being right in the middle of the action. It doesn’t matter what – whether that’s gadding about the globe, changing careers on a whim or picking the most adrenalin-fuelled hobbies – it just has to be exciting.

    And what could be more thrilling than taking on a Hungarian Horntail? Yes, we’re sure Harry would have rather not come face-to-face with this fire-breathing deadly beast that soars through the sky, but it would be your ultimate dream. In the meantime, having this magnificent creature on your Christmas jumper will do.

    If you and your bestie are inseparable… then you’d love Fred and George’s jumpers

    Always finishing each other’s sentences, you and your best friend make us believe in platonic soulmates. You spend every minute you possibly can together and it’s almost like you share one mind. This is why we think the Weasley twins’ coordinating jumpers are perfect for you.

    Firstly, there’s no way that you and your bestie would pass up on the twinning opportunity of matching jumper colours. Yet, you both also appreciate the nod to each of you as individuals with the initials. Secondly, the pair of you have the same energy as Fred and George. You’re loyal to each other. You challenge each other. You make the world a brighter place when you are together.

    Known for making bold fashion choices? Then you’d have Harry’s roarsome jumper

    With your avant-garde outfits in bold and brilliant colours – nobody would call you a wallflower. You embrace your individuality and don’t worry about what other people might think. Forget following trends, clothes are a way for you to express your dazzling personality.

    This is why the jumper that Molly made Harry in Prisoner of Azkaban would suit you. It takes a great deal of bravery to always be unapologetically yourself, so wearing a jumper with the symbol of the house know for this trait is kind of perfect. Made with bright scarlet wool and with a magnificent lion, you’d definitely stand out from the crowd.
