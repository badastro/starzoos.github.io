    Our ultimate Harry Potter Christmas gift guide for 2023

    Published on Nov 10th 2023
    ItIt is surely not that time already? Already?! Yes! Christmas is just around the corner once again - and while we imagine the students of Hogwarts winding down for the festive holidays... we've got some shopping to do! From Christmas jumpers to shiny new books, here's just a few ideas for gifts for that ultimate Harry Potter fan in your life.

    Grab your Galleons and check out some of our recommendations for 2023.

    Snuggly Selections | Light Reading | Stocking Fillers | Figures and Collectibles | Something Spectacular | For the Little Ones | Real-life Experiences | Gifts for Gamers

    Some snuggly selections

    Here's a cosy collection of winter warmers to really settle in for the colder months in wizarding style.

    Hogwarts Christmas Jumper | Harry Potter Shop

    In the words of Ron Weasley, we “need to sort out our priorities” – and what better way to do that than with a classic Hogwarts Christmas jumper? This white snowy number is giving us the festive feels simply by looking at it – for there truly is nowhere like Hogwarts to give us the ultimate Christmas vibe.

    Check out the jumper here.

    Or if you're in the US, find it here.

    Hogwarts house themed pyjamas | Primark (UK only)

    If you’re based in the UK, this latest range of Harry Potter goodies from Primark feature these very cosy looking pyjama sets, themed around your Hogwarts house. Go mellow in yellow in this Hufflepuff number, or find the right house for you to match your snuggly style.

    Pick up a set here.

    Hogwarts Express loungewear | Harry Potter Shop

    We love all of this cuddlesome clothing from the official Harry Potter shop, which has just launched a new loungewear range, all based around the Hogwarts Express and the general joy of starting term at the wizarding school. Why not check out this collection for something comfy, such as bathrobes and snuggly socks.

    Get snuggly!

    Christmas Spirit Jersey

    If you hear the song “Let it Snow”, and instantly think of Hedwig the snowy owl, we’ve got you. This fabulously cheerful red jersey is the perfect colour of Christmastime, and there’s little Hedwig, flying yonder, carrying a present, on the back. Wear this jersey in eyeshot of someone who is being a bit of a Scrooge, and you’ll magically transform them into a Christmas-obsessive.

    Cosy up to this jersey right here.

    Hedwig Hoodie

    Okay, but what if you really love Hedwig... to the extent you want to be Hedwig? Again, we’ve got you! Spread your wings and embrace your inner owl with this extremely cosy-looking onesie. With feather details on the arms, an owl face on the hook, and a cute letter pouch for your owl post, you will be both snuggly and ready to take flight within a moment’s notice.

    Fly on over to the Harry Potter Shop.

    A bit of light reading

    The Harry Potter Wizarding Almanac

    Unless you’ve had your head stuck in a Vanishing Cabinet these past couple of weeks, you have probably heard of this enchanting new compendium of lists, maps, infographics and more: the Harry Potter Wizarding Almanac. Crammed with full-colour spreads, illustrated by a range of talented artists, this book will tell you everything you need to know about the Harry Potter characters, spells and locations – and that’s just the tip of the iceberg.

    Take a step inside the Almanac here.

    You can pick up your own copy from today via Bloomsbury or Scholastic.

    Harry Potter Official Christmas Cookbook

    Are you looking to spruce up your Christmas dinner this year with a touch of magic? Well, the first ever official Harry Potter Christmas Cookbook may just be the ticket. Packed with Wizarding World inspired recipes, from sweet to savoury, why not wow the family with these aesthetically pleasing (and seasonal!) dishes? With full-colour spreads and easy-to-follow instructions, you’ll be whipping up a Weasley jumper focaccia or a Golden Snitch popcorn ball in no time.

    Take a look.

    MinaLima Illustrated Edition of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

    We love seeing the Harry Potter stories re-interpreted in so many fabulous ways, and the design team MinaLima have surpassed themselves with this latest contribution. The iconic third Harry Potter story has been immortalised in MinaLima’s unique style, full of meticulous detail, as ever. Flip through the pages and stumble upon beautiful full-colour drawings of some of your favourite things from this dark third instalment, such as the Knight Bus, Buckbeak the Hippogriff, and much more.

    Pick up a copy here – and even discover a signed edition from the official MinaLima shop!

    And!

    Speaking of MinaLima... Studio MinaLima, in collaboration with Warner Bros. Discovery Global Consumer Products, has created a collection of 11 pieces featuring a pattern based on the Black Family Tapestry, which we see Sirius showing to Harry in Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix.

    MinaLima’s Black Family Tapestry collection will be available in time for Christmas exclusively at House of MinaLima shops: in London from 11th November and in New York from 12th November. The collection will be available internationally for online purchase from minalima.com on the 12th January.

    Stocking fillers

    A shoutout to the more-neurotic gift-givers reading this: sometimes you’re worried someone might not like their main present, so you throw in a few side-presents just in case! And we completely relate. So, here’s a few smaller treats to pop in a stocking, or wrap up as an extra surprise.

    Harry Potter stocking bundle

    Well, first things first! You might need a stocking!

    This lovely wintery one from the Harry Potter shop comes filled with wizarding world presents, just in case you want to make life nice and easy for yourself.

    Eat your heart out, Dobby. Here is the ultimate sock.

    Here's the UK version.
    Here's the US version!

    Harry Potter nail kits | Glamnetic

    Hogwarts school isn’t often known for its beauty secrets (the fact Professor Snape doesn’t seem to wash his hair says it all) but you can absolutely ooze Harry Potter glamour with these fabulous nail kits from Glamnetic. Coming in Hogwarts house-colours or dainty press-on designs, glam up your claws with these magical additions in different shapes and sizes.

    Nailed it!

    Harry Potter candles | Short Story

    You can’t go wrong with a festive candle as a gift - but what if these candles were adorned with your favourite Harry Potter characters, in adorable kawaii form, in cheerful colours? Your wish is our command. This delightful range comes from Short Story, and features characters such as Draco Malfoy (scented cocoa and espresso flavour!) and Luna Lovegood (scented blackberry and Nargle taffy!!) among others. Each mini-candle, which has been lovingly handcrafted with an aroma that suits the character, also comes with an enamel pin to make this gift extra-special.

    Take a look at the full candle collection.

    Ooh - and as if by magic, we have a little discount for you on this one. For a limited time only, enjoy 20% off Short Story’s entire Harry Potter collection - including brand new mini candles, air fresheners and bookmarks - using the code: HPFANCLUB

    Offer ends 10 December 2023, subject to availability, while stocks last.

    Go figure

    This selection of figures, dolls and toys has got us nostalgic for our childhoods – not that you’re ever too old for some of these immaculate models, capturing key Wizarding World characters.

    Harry Potter x Mattel

    These new (quite realistic looking!) dolls from Mattel are bringing your favourite characters to life in a whole new way, created to celebrate Warner Bros. 100th anniversary. At 12 inches tall, complete with accessories, you’ll love having these iconic characters on display in your home.

    You can check out the Harry figure and the Lord Voldemort figure right here.

    Interactive Dobby

    Relive the magic of Harry Potter with Dobby, our favorite house-elf! Coming with 30 sounds and phrases, this interactive house-elf can move his ears, blink, lift his arms and move his head – and he even comes with his very own sock.

    Pick him up here.

    Hogwarts House Bears | Hamleys, UK only

    Our hearts! If you’re based in the UK, these positively adorable furry friends are available in four different Hogwarts house styles. The lovely teddies are equipped with colour-coded scarves, and the iconic Hamleys logo on their paw. We just want to give them a hug right now!

    All four bears are available in Gryffindor style, Ravenclaw style, Hufflepuff style or Slytherin style respectively!

    LEGO Hogwarts Castle and grounds

    This year, The LEGO Group went above and beyond to represent the Wizarding World in the most innovative of fashions. This resplendent Hogwarts castle and grounds set proves just how creative you can be with LEGO, coming in at 2660 pieces. Watch out for all the little details too – such as a hidden Chamber of Secrets and a certain mischievous blue car caught in the Whomping Willow.

    Take a closer look.

    LEGO Dobby

    If you’re not too exhausted from building an entire Hogwarts, how about building a house-elf to help you? This lovely interpretation of Dobby comes with buildable accessories, such as the book where Dobby finds his famous sock in – and even Mrs Dursley’s delicious pudding that meets a messy ending in Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets. Thankfully, this LEGO version is far more intact.

    Here he is!

    Something spectacular

    If you’re looking for something extra-enchanting this year, here’s a gift idea to give that special someone in your life the same facial expression as Harry Potter receiving his Firebolt.

    Harry & Hagrid trunks (US only)

    Perhaps the Harry Potter fan in your life has absolutely everything already and you’re stuck? Not to fear… these brand-new gift trunks, themed around Harry and Hagrid respectively, could really up the ante! Packed with magical goodies, from sweet treats to a festive mug, all packed up in a rustic Hogwarts-style trunk, you’re going to be very popular at Christmastime indeed!

    Pick up the Hagrid trunk here.

    Or the Harry trunk here.

    Harry Potter x Roo & You Joey Play Couch

    Whether your imagination takes you to napping in the Gryffindor common room or hanging out in Hagrid's Hut, this new Harry Potter collab with Roo & You unlocks a magical world of cozy, creative and imaginative play. Adorned in all the best Harry Potter iconography, this cheerful sofa comes in many different designs and colours – the kids will love it.

    Bounce on over!

    Butterbeer backpacks | Loungefly

    These imaginative mini-backpacks (based on the beautiful designs of MinaLima) really capture a more retro vibe, celebrating the Wizarding World’s most beloved beverage. Crafted in a high quality PU leather material in vibrant colours, you’ll be the bell of Hogsmeade in this little number..

    Shop here for the UK and here for the US.

    Baby clothes | Kyte Baby

    What about a gift for those budding witches and wizards in your life? Begin a young one's magical education with this gorgeous selection of blankets, bodysuits, bibs and rompers from Kyte Baby - we especially love that Herbology design for those little Mandrakes out there.

    Take a look!

    Real-life experiences

    Sometimes, when it comes to the season of giving, you might want to go a little deeper – gifting someone a great memory rather than something you unwrap. These Wizarding World experiences may be the perfect way to surprise someone in a larger-than-life sort of way!

    Warner Bros. Studio Tour London souvenir gift tickets

    The home of the Harry Potter films has perfectly crystallised the movie magic of the series – with full sets of some of your favourite Wizarding World locations. So why not gift this experience to someone with these souvenir gift tickets?

    The tickets are inspired by the Hogwarts acceptance letter. The envelope is addressed to Mr. H. Potter at number 4 Privet Drive, (we’re sure you’re familiar with him!) and when unfolded, reveals a beautifully crafted letter personalised to your gift recipient, including a short message from the gift giver.

    Explore the Studio Tour!

    If you’re based in Japan, you might have spotted that a new version of the Studio Tour has now opened in Tokyo this year – complete with some special additions you can’t get anywhere else in the world. You can read our full lowdown here – or grab a ticket or two for Tokyo now.

    Give the gift of Harry Potter and the Cursed Child

    In London

    Gift someone a visit to the award-winning Harry Potter and the Cursed Child, now in its eighth year in London’s West End.

    19 years after Harry, Ron, and Hermione saved the wizarding world, they’re back on a most extraordinary new adventure – this time, joined by a brave new generation that has only just arrived at the legendary Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Prepare for spectacular spells, a mind-blowing race through time, and an epic battle to stop mysterious forces, all while the future hangs in the balance.

    For more information and to purchase a gift card, visit HarryPotterTheatreVouchers.co.uk

    And beyond

    Of course, the award-winning play is also playing in other territories as we speak, such as Tokyo, New York and Hamburg. You can check the global Cursed Child site to pick up tickets for that special someone.

    You can also sign-up for updates here.

    Harry Potter London Breaks

    If you’re feeling extra-swish, you can go one step beyond with your experiential London Harry Potter gift, wrapping up even more magic in one friendly bundle. Harry Potter London Breaks offers fans the chance to see the Warner Bros. Studio Tour London, Cursed Child play and grab a hotel all in one fell swoop.

    Explore Harry Potter London Breaks.

    Harry Potter: A Yule Ball Celebration (Mexico City, Santiago de Chile)

    An unforgettable evening themed around the Yule Ball in Goblet of Fire, is still available to book in certain territories. So, if you know someone in Mexico City or Santiago de Chile who fancies a wizarding waltz, let them know about this spectacular (not to mention festive) event.

    Dance on over the official Yule Ball Celebration ticket website.

    Harry Potter: Visions of Magic (Cologne, Germany)

    New for 2024, this ethereal art experience promises to take you to mysterious Wizarding World locations through interactive, innovative ways. Opening in Germany, tickets are available to purchase now. Are you ready to illuminate the invisible? All will be revealed soon!

    Explore Visions of Magic tickets.

    Games

    Hogwarts Legacy

    Earlier this year, Hogwarts Legacy rocked our worlds by introducing us to the wizarding school in the most detailed way we’d ever seen, via the medium of this epic, open-world RPG. Do you know someone who hasn’t played it yet who has been dying to visit Hogwarts? This gift could give them the closest thing to that desire.

    The game is currently available on PlayStation®5, PlayStation®4, PlayStation®4 Pro, Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One consoles and PC, but is also coming soon to Nintendo Switch on the 14th November.

    Perhaps you already know someone who has the game but wants to dig into it even deeper? Bloomsbury released “The Art and Making of Hogwarts Legacy” this year which should absolutely scratch that itch. Take a look.
