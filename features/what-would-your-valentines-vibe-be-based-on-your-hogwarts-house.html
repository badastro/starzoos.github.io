    What would your Valentine's vibe be based on your Hogwarts house?

    Published on Feb 12th 2026
    WithWith Professor Lockhart’s favourite season upon us, it’s time to celebrate love! Yet how would the different houses approach this romantic holiday? We’ve had a think and come up with their Valentine’s vibes…

    As a Gryffindor, your Valentine’s Day is all about the grand gesture

    Known for being bold and daring, as a Gryffindor, you channel that same energy when it comes to Valentine’s Day. You’re not just going to settle for a generic card or a lacklustre bunch of flowers picked up at the petrol station. No, like Fred and George sailing away from Hogwarts on the back of their brooms or Ron sacrificing himself on the giant wizard chess board, you’re all about the grand gesture. From starring in a flash mob to professing your love through skywriting, it’s go big or go home.

    The Hufflepuff Valentine’s vibe is wholesome and homemade

    Loyal and unafraid of hard work, like Professor Sprout diligently tending to her plethora of magical plants so they bloom, you pour your heart and soul into creating the perfect present for your Valentine. You won’t find a gift hastily grabbed from any old shop. In fact, whether you’re baking your crush’s favourite cake or carefully cutting and sticking photos of you and your one true love into a romantic scrapbook, you’re all about that wholesome and homemade Valentine’s vibe.

    For Ravenclaw, Valentine’s Day is all about love letters and words of affection

    The house of wit and wisdom is home to some of the most imaginative thinkers – such as Luna Lovegood. She had a unique way of seeing the world and a wonderful way with words (who can forget her brilliant Quidditch commentary). This is a trait that you embody too, which is why your Valentine’s vibe is all about creative expression. In particular, the art of writing love letters. As a wise wordsmith, you’ll find no clunky comparisons to eyes being as green as a fresh pickled toad or hair as dark as blackboard in your writing, just the perfect formula of words that create their own romantic magic.

    It’s all about the perfect luxury gift for a Slytherin Valentine

    As a member of Slytherin house you are known for your ambition. You don’t settle for anything less than you think you deserve. Like Draco Malfoy and his Nimbus 2001, you always want to have the very best and are drawn to the finer things in life. With that in mind, you believe that the person you love deserves the world too. This is why your Valentine’s are all about the flashy gifts and experiences – whisking your partner away on an expensive holiday, buying them the most dazzling jewels or finding that perfect gift you think is just as special as they are.
